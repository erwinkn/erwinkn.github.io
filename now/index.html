Now

I’m currently working with Vicat on how to get to net-zero emissions in the cement industry. With them, I’m building N-Zero, a modelling tool to create long term roadmaps towards net-zero CO2 emissions. While the project is private, I wrote a primer on the cement & concrete industries from a climate lens here.

I recently caught the deep learning bug as well. Right now, I’m working on mini-dl, an educational deep learning framework built on top of JAX, and simple-grad, an introduction going from zero to a complete auto-differentiation system, similar to what PyTorch or JAX use under the hood.

I’m also very interested in anything related to reducing emissions in the construction industry and additive manufacturing (3D printing). If you’re an expert on any of those topics, please reach out!
