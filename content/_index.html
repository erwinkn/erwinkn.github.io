Project in-residence: the Munsell solid

The first time I saw a representation of the Munsell color system, I was mesmerized. It was the first color system to separate hue, value and chroma (now commonly used in HSL and HSV) into three independent dimensions. Roughly, hue is the color, value is the brightness (white has a value of 0) and chroma is the saturation.
